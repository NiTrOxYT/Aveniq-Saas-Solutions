# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Agency administrators, founders, and project leads managing sales pipelines, client outreach, rate limits, serverless logging, email analytics, portfolio projects, and security controls.

## Product Purpose
Provide a high-fidelity, unified internal CRM and operations control deck for Aveniq agency. Centralizes lead ingestion, submissions review, email dispatch auditing, portfolio curating, live analytics telemetry, and system security monitoring.

## Brand Personality
Sleek, precise, structural, high-contrast, engineering-focused. Linear-style clean interfaces with Stripe-level information density.

## Anti-references
- AI-purple glowing grids
- Centered grid cards with large round circles
- Colored border-stripe accents on cards
- Low contrast, fuzzy text styling
- Slow animations

## Design Principles
1. **Utility First**: Design serves user efficiency. Clear tables, quick filters, fast transitions.
2. **Dense but Readable**: High data density with comfortable spacing (cockpit view).
3. **Impeccable Hierarchy**: Typographic contrast over card-nesting blocks.
4. **Resilient & Optimistic**: Instant status feedback and error resiliency.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio ≥4.5:1 for all text.
- Full keyboard accessibility.
- Support `prefers-reduced-motion` settings.
